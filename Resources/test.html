Summary: Latitude vs. X

Does it get hotter as you get closer to the equator? Yes, of course it does! The purpose of this project was to prove this by analyzing data from 500 cities, which was pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API.

After compiling and cleaning the dataset, we used pandas and Matplotlib to review the data. While we know that the temperature increases as you get closer to the equator, we plotted this out to showcase the relationship. We also took a look at a few other weather data details to review how they relate to latitude: cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. This site provides the source data, the visualizations, and the analysis of our findings.
